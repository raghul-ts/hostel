Nutrient-Rich Meal Plan

Day 1

Breakfast:

Greek yogurt parfait with mixed berries, chia seeds, and a drizzle of honey.

Lunch:

Quinoa salad with roasted vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes) and a lemon-tahini dressing.

Dinner:

Baked salmon with steamed broccoli and wild rice.

Day 2

Breakfast:

Spinach and feta omelette with whole grain toast.

Lunch:

Lentil soup with a side of mixed green salad topped with avocado slices and balsamic vinaigrette.

Dinner:

Grilled chicken breast with sweet potato wedges and sautéed spinach.

Day 3

Breakfast:

Overnight oats made with almond milk, sliced banana, walnuts, and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Lunch:

Turkey and avocado wrap with whole grain tortilla, served with carrot sticks and hummus.

Dinner:

Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, snap peas, and carrots) in a ginger-soy sauce, served over brown rice.

Day 4

Breakfast:

Smoothie made with spinach, banana, frozen berries, almond milk, and a scoop of protein powder.

Lunch:

Quinoa and black bean stuffed bell peppers topped with salsa and a side of mixed greens.

Dinner:

Grilled shrimp skewers with quinoa pilaf and roasted asparagus.

Day 5

Breakfast:

Whole grain toast topped with mashed avocado and sliced tomato, served with a side of scrambled eggs.

Lunch:

Chickpea salad with cucumber, tomato, red onion, parsley, and a lemon-tahini dressing.

Dinner:

Baked cod with roasted Brussels sprouts and a quinoa salad with cranberries and almonds.

Day 6

Breakfast:

Greek yogurt smoothie bowl topped with granola, sliced strawberries, and a drizzle of honey.

Lunch:

Whole grain pasta salad with grilled vegetables (such as eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers) tossed in a pesto vinaigrette.

Dinner:

Beef and vegetable stir-fry with broccoli, bell peppers, and snap peas served over brown rice.

Day 7

Breakfast:

Veggie scramble with sautéed spinach, bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms, served with whole grain toast.

Lunch:

Spinach and quinoa salad with roasted butternut squash, dried cranberries, and a balsamic vinaigrette.

Dinner:

Baked chicken thighs with roasted root vegetables (such as carrots, parsnips, and potatoes) and a side of steamed green beans.
